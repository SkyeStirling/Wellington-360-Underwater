Discover Wellington's Rocky Reefs

The reefs inside our fully protected marine reserve are buzzing with life. Dive into schools of fish, hover above amazing seaweed beds while you observe all the action around you and get eye-to-eye with curious fish and resident rock lobsters ('crayfish'). Taputeranga Marine reserve is a fantastic place to visit both in and out of the water

HMNZS Wellington (F69) VR Tour

HMNZS Wellington (F69) was deliberately sunk off the South Coast in 2005. The shipwreck now lies within the Taputeranga Marine Reserve and provides an artificial reef on sandy and cobbly bottom in about 21 meters.
