HMNZS Wellington (F69) VR Tour

HMNZS Wellington (F69) was deliberately sunk off the South Coast in 2005. The shipwreck now lies within the Taputeranga Marine Reserve and provides an artificial reef on sandy and cobbly bottom in about 21 meters.
